Connections. ProtoPedia has support for diagrams in which shapes or text boxes are connected by lines or arrows. In this sort of diagram, some items are "nodes" and some are "connectors" (and some are neither). Elements of the shape catalog are flagged as to which role they play (eg circles, rectangles, and text boxes of various kinds are nodes, and lines and arrows are connectors). If you insert and select something with the node role, the left hand panel will show an option to "connect". If you click connect, and then select another node, an arrow from the first to the second item will appear. If you subsequently drag the nodes around, the arrow will be adjusted to maintain the connection. Subsequently, arrows may be replaced by any element with the connector role.
